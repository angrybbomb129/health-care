Home » Our Services » Skilled Nursing

Skilled and compassionate nurses at your service

We provide highly qualified Registered Nurses and Licensed Vocational Nurses who offer skilled care and essential instructions. This enables our patients to function independently and increase their comfort and overall quality of life. Also, the patient’s primary physician is kept in close communication to ensure the delivery of health care services customized to meet the needs of each individual.

Such services include but are not limited to:

  • Treatments and Injections
  • Supervision of Medications
  • Wound Care/ Wound Assessment
  • Ostomy Care
  • Dressing Changes
  • Catheter Care
  • Restorative Care
  • Simple Pulmo-physiotherapy
  • Family/patient Health Teaching and Counseling
  • Diabetic Teachings
  • Drawing Blood & Lab Work
  • Intravenous Infusions
  • Pre-Op and Post- Op Care
  • Inter Facility Referral

Mission Statement

Our commitment is to always keep our clients and their families our priority by providing them with exceptional healthcare services in their homes.

At Angeleno Home Health Care, Inc, our patients enjoy the convenience of a hassle-free medical visit. Because our greatest goal is to guarantee satisfaction, we have finely trained personnel working around the clock and provide the highest quality of health care possible.

Angeleno Home Health Care, Inc combines leading-edge knowledge with dedication. Our regular visits and personalized appointments keep our patients constantly surrounded by respectable personnel and working head to meet their needs. These are people who like helping others and who make themselves accessible to devoting long hours to provide superior care services.

With a rich diversity of ethnicities and nationalities, Angeleno Home Health Care, Inc entertains an unsurpassed environment of friendliness and mutual respect. Furthermore, we are happy to accommodate the multi-cultural nature of the Los Angeles area by providing staff fluent in a variety of languages such as Spanish, Armenian, Russian, Tagalog, Farsi, and English

We also coordinate durable medical equipment and appliances for our patients’ use and comfort.
